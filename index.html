FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

01. Is Intworks a Recruitment Agency?

No, Intworks Ltd is not a recruitment agency. Our focus and mission revolve around providing innovative healthcare integration solutions. We firmly believe that the healthcare sector, including the NHS and other organizations, should not be burdened with increased charges. Our core objective is to prioritize the well-being of patients and to contribute to the improvement of healthcare services through our expertise in integration.

02. What are your prices?

At Intworks, we offer customized pricing tailored to each specific project's requirements. Our rates are determined based on a comprehensive evaluation of the scope, complexity, and specific needs involved. For less complex services, we provide costings that are typically lower compared to complex architectural services.

We take pride in our commitment to providing competitive pricing that ensures our services remain affordable and cost-effective for our clients. In fact, we guarantee that our pricing will always be more favorable when compared to recruitment agencies.

Rest assured that when you choose Intworks, you not only benefit from our exceptional healthcare integration solutions but also from our commitment to delivering value for your investment. Contact us today to discuss your project requirements and receive a personalized pricing proposal that meets your specific needs.

03. What projects have you completed?

From connecting two medical systems and establishing single channel services with basic mapping to seamlessly integrating with global CRM systems using REST APIs, we have successfully completed numerous simple integration projects. Our experience expands into the complex realm of healthcare integration, where we have proven expertise in seamlessly integrating RIS and PACS systems with other vital components of healthcare organizations.

Whether it's bridging gaps between different systems or ensuring efficient information flow within the healthcare ecosystem, we have the knowledge and capabilities to make it happen. With our deep understanding of healthcare integration intricacies, we provide comprehensive solutions that enhance connectivity, streamline processes, and improve overall efficiency.

At Intworks, we are committed to delivering exceptional integration services tailored to the unique needs of each client. Partner with us and benefit from our vast experience and industry-leading solutions, transforming your healthcare organization into a well-connected and high-performing entity.

04. But why Intworks?

Throughout our extensive experience in the healthcare industry, we have identified a recurring challenge in complex projects: communication breakdowns between different organizations. Recognizing the significance of effective collaboration, we have developed a proactive approach to address this issue.

In our interactions, we prioritize open and transparent communication, fostering an environment where all stakeholders feel comfortable asking questions and expressing concerns. We understand that speaking up in meetings can be daunting, especially when key decision-makers are involved. That's why we strive to create a safe space where everyone feels empowered to contribute.

Moreover, we recognize the importance of avoiding technical jargon that can hinder effective communication. Our team excels at translating complex concepts into clear and concise language, ensuring that all parties involved have a solid understanding of the project objectives and processes.

Above all, we approach each project with empathy, recognizing the diverse perspectives and interests of all stakeholders. By actively listening and considering everyone's needs, we promote a collaborative atmosphere and facilitate smooth project execution.

At Intworks, we believe that effective communication is the backbone of successful project management. Through our empathetic and inclusive approach, we streamline complex healthcare projects, fostering efficiency, and ensuring that each stakeholder's voice is heard and valued.
